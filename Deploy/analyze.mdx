# Call History

The **Call History** page provides a comprehensive log of all inbound calls received by your agent. Key features include:

- **Call Duration** – View the length of each call.
- **Call Sentiment** – Understand the tone of the conversation.
- **Call Summary** – Quickly access a brief overview of the call.
- **Full Call Recording** – Listen to the complete recording for review.
- **Export to CSV** – Download call data for offline analysis or reporting.
