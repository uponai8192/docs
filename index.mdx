## Building your first agent (broad overview)

1. Login to your account provided for you  
2. Create a new Agent  
3. Navigate to the **Agents** tab  
4. Select **Manage Agents**  
5. Click **Create from Template**  
6. Choose from one of our templates and create your agent  

## Test Your Agent

Click **Start Test Call** to see your agent in action.

Check out our in-depth guides to learn how to customize and implement your agent.
